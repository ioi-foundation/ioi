IOI Slate Cobalt

Slate surfaces, cobalt signal.

White and slate in light mode, deep navy and electric blue in dark mode. This captures the original IOI palette: crisp slate SaaS surfaces in light mode, deep navy theatre in dark mode, and cobalt blue for signal.

Light foundation: #ffffff, #f8fafc, #0f172a, #2563eb. Dark foundation: #000510, #050608, #ffffff, #4a9eff.
